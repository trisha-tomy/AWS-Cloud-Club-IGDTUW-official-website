FAQ

What is AWS?

AWS, or Amazon Web Services, is a cloud computing platform provided by Amazon. It offers a wide range of services like computing power, storage, networking, and databases, enabling businesses to scale without heavy infrastructure investment.

Why use AWS?

AWS offers flexibility, scalability, cost-effectiveness, reliability, and security. Businesses can scale resources as needed, pay only for what they use, and rely on a secure infrastructure maintained by AWS.

How does AWS pricing work?

AWS operates on a pay-as-you-go model. Pricing varies based on services used, region, and usage metrics like data transfer and compute time. Discounts are available for long-term commitments and reserved instances.
